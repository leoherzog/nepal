The Story

 

We're going to Nepal!

Leo and I have been attending Engedi Church for about 5 years now. Mission work has been at the heart of the church since itsâ€™ beginning, and it has been something that we have intentionally been getting more involved with recently. While on an Engedi mission trip to Nepal last April, I (Megan) befriended the one-and-only other girl on the adventure, Erika. During our time there, it became extremely clear that she was to sell her things and move to Nepal. As she prepared to leave, Erika asked me and Leo to be her U.S. support team and we humbly accepted.

Erika is working for an organization called Beauty for Ashes Nepal (BFAN). BFAN is a business with a mission to employ marginalized women at risk for being exploited, as well as women rescued from the sex-industry, and share with them the love of Jesus. In Nepal, jobs for women are extremely limited, especially for women who have been exploited in the sex-industry. BFAN trains these vulnerable women how to sew and/or create jewelry, which BFAN then sells to other organizations or exports to Western countries. The women are then employed at a livable-wage with a retirement account, medical reimbursement, and scholarships for their children to go to school. Through business, the women are able to provide for their families with dignity!

As we have been corresponding with Erika over the past few months while she has been transitioning to life in Nepal, it has become clear that BFAN could use some help with the technology side of the business. Many of their computer programs and practices are out of date or not working, so we have been asked to come help! We will be going for two weeks, leaving on February 14th. Leo will be taking care of the technology needs, and I will be assisting with physical needs and pouring into/encouraging Erika emotionally and spiritually.

The work being done in Nepal is something we really want to be a part of and truly feel called by God to go. In order for Leo and me to go on this trip, we need to raise $3,000. Please consider partnering with us financially by donating to our efforts. Please pray and take your time considering how you can join us on this mission.

 

With lots of love!

Megan & Leo
